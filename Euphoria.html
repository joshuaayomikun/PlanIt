Euphoria Event Place

About
Euphoria Event Place at full capacity sits 1000 guests in a banquet setting and 1200 guests in a theater setting. It sits on about one acre parcel of land, therefore providing adequate parking facility for the convenience and comfort of our guests. The Marquee is fully air-conditioned and comes with comfortable banquet chairs and tables and wooden floors which enhances sound to the delight of performing musicians.
