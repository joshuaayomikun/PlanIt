Royal Event Center

About
Royal event centre is a ground floor multi purpose event centre for all event situated in Ikeja with a capacity of 180 guests (banquet). The hall is fully equipped with AC and complete sound system. We also have excellent parking space, food court, changing room, standby generator, 24hours security and a bookshop..
